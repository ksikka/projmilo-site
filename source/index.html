Our mission

Project Milo is a network of tech-savvy students, hailing from universities and high schools across the nation. We breathe life into used computers to tackle problems in education and healthcare.

Project Milo offers professional computer repair services for businesses and consumers. We donate nearly half the proceeds to sponsor life-saving surgeries for children. In addition, we re-purpose donated computers and donate them to budget-strapped public schools, where they serve as a vital tools in education.
